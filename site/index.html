06 — Details

Shipping,
care & ownership.

Made to order

Each vessel is carved on commission from a single block — 25 days to 18 weeks depending on the stone. Nothing is held in stock; nothing is repeated.

Worldwide freight, paid by client

Crated and shipped by white-glove sea freight. Indicative door-to-door freight runs $1,400–$2,800 by piece and destination, arranged at cost and settled by the buyer.

Delivery & installation

Delivered to the threshold and placed by our freight team. Plumbing is connected by your licensed installer; we provide rough-in dimensions on reservation.

Care of the stone

Sealed natural stone. Clean with a pH-neutral soap and soft cloth; re-seal every 12–18 months. With care, a vessel holds a century of water.

Payment & reservation

Reserve online; a secure payment link and freight quote follow within 24 hours. Deposit terms are available for commissioned pieces.

Guarantee

Every piece is inspected before dispatch and insured in transit. A ten-year structural guarantee accompanies each vessel.
