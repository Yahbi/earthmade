VDetails

Shipping,
care & ownership.

Made to order

Each vessel is carved on commission from a single block — 4 to 20 weeks depending on the stone. Reserve online; a secure payment link and freight figure follow within 24 hours. Deposit terms available.

Freight — we arrange, you pay

Every vessel is crated and shipped door-to-door by white-glove freight that we manage on your behalf and fully insure in transit to full value. A flat regional freight & insurance charge is added at checkout and paid by the client — see the table below.

Delivery & installation

Delivered to the threshold and placed by our freight team. Plumbing is connected by your licensed installer; we provide rough-in dimensions on reservation.

Care & guarantee

Sealed natural stone — clean with pH-neutral soap; re-seal every 12–18 months. Every piece is inspected before dispatch and carries a ten-year structural guarantee.

Delivered and fully insured worldwide. A fixed freight & insurance figure — typically $1,900–$3,400 by region — is confirmed with your reservation and settled by the client.
